Meats

We offer a variety of meats, each prepared with authentic flavors. All our meats are marinated with traditional Mexican spices and grilled to perfection.


  • Carne Asada – Grilled marinated steak
  • Pollo Asado – Grilled chicken
  • Carnitas – Slow‑cooked shredded pork
  • Al Pastor – Marinated pork cooked with pineapple
  • Barbacoa – Slow‑braised beef
  • Birria – Stewed beef cooked with chiles and spices
  • Chorizo – Spicy Mexican sausage
  • Tripa – Fried or grilled beef intestines
  • Lengua – Tender beef tongue
  • Cabeza – Beef head meat
  • Pescado – Grilled or fried fish
  • Camarón – Shrimp grilled or sautéed
